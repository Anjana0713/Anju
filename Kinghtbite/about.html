Knight Bite is a cloud kitchen which delivers food to the nocturnals, giving equal emphasis on food, technology, and logistics.

Knight Bite started with the idea to serve all the hungry nocturnals who find it really difficult to order late-night munchies. KB brought together late-night eaters and helped them order delicious meals at their convenience via the Knight Bite App and call.

Knight Bite is one such concept which was highly successful in adapting to the emerging market. Biters accepted and loved the KB app that made the ordering process much easier.

Going forward, our aim is to help and bring smiles to many more faces and expand to many more cities across India and around the world.
